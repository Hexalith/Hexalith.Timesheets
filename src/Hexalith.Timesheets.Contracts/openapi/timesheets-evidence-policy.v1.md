# Timesheets Evidence Policy Guidance

Timesheets treats comments as sensitive unstructured evidence by default.

## Retention Categories

- TimeEntryEvidence: Time Entry evidence is retained by default.
- CommentText: Comment retention policy is unresolved.
- ExportRecord: Export record retention requires tenant policy.
- MagicLinkConfirmationAuditMetadata: Confirmation audit metadata retention is unresolved.

## Comment Rules

- Comments may contain sensitive information.
- Comments may be excluded by policy.
- Comment export is disabled until policy allows it.
- Comments are excluded from diagnostics.

## Launch Readiness Gaps

- Legal-hold policy is unresolved.
- Tenant-specific retention overrides are unresolved.
- Comment sensitivity policy is unresolved.
